Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

Stroku is designed with privacy in mind. Our apps operate locally on your network and do not collect personal information or transmit data to external servers.

How We Use Information

The Stroku apps only use network information locally to:

  • Discover Roku devices on your local network
  • Establish streaming connections between devices
  • Stream content directly from your Android device to your Roku

Data Storage

All data remains on your local devices. No information is stored on external servers or transmitted to third parties.

Network Usage

The apps communicate only within your local WiFi network to enable streaming functionality.

Open Source

Both Stroku apps are open source and available on GitHub. You can review the code to verify our privacy practices.
