How It Works

Three simple steps to start streaming

1

Install Both Apps

Download Stroku Sender on your Android device and install Stroku Receiver on your Roku device.

2

Connect Devices

Ensure both devices are on the same WiFi network. The sender will automatically discover your Roku.

3

Start Streaming

Select your content in Stremio and use the Stroku Sender to stream it directly to your Roku device.
